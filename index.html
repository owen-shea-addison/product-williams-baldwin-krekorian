ABOUT the toothpaste

Unpaste Zero Waste is a new and improved eco-friendly toothpaste. The toothpaste tablet is easy to use and to travel - with the fact that is leaves behind no mess! With the application of one tablet on your brush, you have the ability to achieve clean teeth and fresh breath for hours. The Unpaste Zero Waste toothpaste has been proven to lead to better dental care over the typical toothpastes that most use. Not only is it great for your dental care, but it is also free of preservatives and gluten as well as it is vegan and non gmo. This is a no mess toothpaste that leaves your breath feeling fresh even after you brush!
